Repetition is emphasized as a key technique to harness the subconscious mind's transformative abilities. The book "The Power of your subconscious mind" by Joseph Murphy, underscores the importance of consistently affirming positive beliefs and desires, asserting that repeated thoughts can deeply influence one's behavior and shape the course of life.

This web app is developed by Kritesh Thapa to help people who wants to apply the repetition process from the book "The Power of your subconscious mind". This app keeps the records of what does user want to repeat, the day when the user has started to use this app, streak, total repetiton, and repetiton on that day.

No data is colleced. Your all data is stored in your browser and you can clear your data any time from the setting.
